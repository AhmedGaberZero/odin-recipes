Strawberry Cake Recipe

Strawberry Cake

This strawberry cake tastes like it came straight from a bakery â€” but it's surprisingly easy to make at home!

Ingredients:

  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Jell-O
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Milk
  • Strawberries
  • Vanilla

Steps:

  1. Beat the butter, sugar, and jell-O together. Add the eggs.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients.
  3. Put the dry mixture into the wet mixture with milk.
  4. Add remaining ingredients and transfere to cake pan.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven until a toothpick comes out clean.
