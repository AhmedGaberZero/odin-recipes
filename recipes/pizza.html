Pizza Recipe

Pizza

Learn how to make the best pizza crust of your life with just a few ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Mix the ingredients.
  2. Let the ough come to room temperature.
  3. Prepare your surface with oil.
  4. Shape the Dough.
  5. Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown.
